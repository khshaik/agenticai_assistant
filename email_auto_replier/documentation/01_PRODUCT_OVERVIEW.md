# Gmail Auto-Replier - Product Overview

> **Intelligent email draft generation powered by Zapier, OpenAI, and AI Agents**

---

## Executive Summary

The **Gmail Auto-Replier** is an intelligent email automation system that leverages AI agents and Zapier's no-code platform to automatically generate contextual draft replies to incoming emails. This enterprise-grade solution enables organizations to automate email response drafting, reducing manual work while maintaining personalized, professional communication.

**Key Value**: Reduce email response time by 80% while maintaining quality and personalization.
